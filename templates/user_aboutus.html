About Us

Alegria- The Festival of joy congregated by The Pillai Group Of Institutions. Alegria is one of the most phenomenal intercollegiate festivals of its kind. It is held in February every year with a mob of more than 50,000 to witness the flow of immense talent in the events. With fun and joy this event also escorts the presence of splendid talent and star celebrities. This festival continues to add a pack of memories to everyone's life with each passing year and the dedicated team of students and faculty always hold its back to add on to the astonishing experience. Be it the unique themes like the “Light The Night” from last year or the astounding performances, they never fail to amaze us. Star celebrities have always been one of the emphasizing parts of the event, this festival has always been bestowed with the presence of some of the most renowned celebrities in the entertainment industry. To name a few, Ranbir kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Ayushman Khurrana, Siddharth Malhotra, Sonam Kapoor, Karthik Aryan, Nushrat Bharucha, Sunny Nijhar, Govinda, Elli Avram, Saquib Salim, Zayed Khan, Rakul Preet Kaur, Lauren Gottlieb, Jackky Bhagani, Ranvijay Singha, Pallavi Sharda, Warina Hussain, Pushkar Jog, Sarah Jane Diad, Aaparshakti Khurrana, Varun Dhawan, Shraddha Kapoor, Aisha Sharma, Shama Sikandar, Rinku Rajguru, Chinmay Udgirkar, Sonali Seygall, Adah Sharma, Rohan Mehra, Nibedita Pal, Chetna Pande along with Indian beatbox sensation D cypher and dance guru Rahul Shetty and Dhanashree Varma and one of the best standup comedian, Kenny Sebastian have blessed us with their presence on the platform that is Alegria to promote their movies and upcoming projects to the magnificent crowd attending and also to judge those events. As it is said, music is a therapy for mind and heart and Alegria was overjoyed to hear the delightful performances for some of the spectacular music artist like Guru Randhawa, Divine, Yo Yo Honey Singh, Superstar Farhan Akhtar, award-winning Romanian pop band Akcent, Dhvani Bhanushali, Armaan Malik and the Sabali Band. Sports world champions Bhaichung Bhutiya, Sunil Chhetri, Ashwini Ponnappa, Sahil Khan, Sangram Chougule, Murali Kumar Syed Kirmani and Rushabh Choksi also visited to display their talent. Alegria experiences some of the finest DJs like Mariana Bo, Tomorrowland’s resident DJ Yves V, DJs Sultan Shepard, DJ Mag’s 33rd ranked Quintino, Teri Miko, Mike Candys and Diego Miranda. The audience can never suppress the urge to trip, spin and sway on their amazing beats. The festival is the outcome of the immense efforts of the students who manage, participate and organize the events taking place in the festival and handle it with great professionalism, spirit and care. Their enthusiasm adds into the festival making it a big success every year. The festival commenced with an ambition of showcasing the power of the youth and has persistently succeeded in doing so.
