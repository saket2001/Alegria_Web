ALEGRIA 2022

Alegria - The Festival joy congregated by The Pillai Group Of Institutions. Alegria is one of the most phenomenal intercollegiate festivals of its kind. It is held in February every year with a mob of more than 50,000 1to witness the flow of immense talent in the events. With fun and joy this event also escorts the presence of splendid talent and star celebrities. This festival continues to add a pack of memories to everyone's life with each passing year and the dedicated team of students and faculty always hold its back to add on to the astonishing experience. Be it the unique themes like the “Light The Night” from last year or the astounding performances, they never fail to amaze us.
